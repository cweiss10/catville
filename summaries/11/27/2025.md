1. The team includes Andy, Samantha, Caroline, Peter, Mei, Diego, Noor, and Leo.

3. The cat mural will incorporate sunrise seeds, a type of plant, in its design as suggested by Noor and Diego.
4. They also plan to create an improvised symphony related to the cat mural, with Leo taking the lead on the music.
5. Some members bring chamomile tea for stray cats at their meetings, inspired by Caroline's suggestion.
6. The team values collaboration and brainstorming, often discussing ideas during gatherings under an old oak tree or improbable amps (musical equipment).
7. They aim to make the project something that the town will cherish.
